# AI Chatbot SaaS Development Plan

## Overview
Complete 20-week development roadmap for building an AI Chatbot SaaS platform that allows businesses to create, deploy, and manage AI-powered chatbots.

## Estimated Timeline: 20 weeks (5 months)
## Estimated Budget: $15,000 - $25,000 (depending on team size and infrastructure costs)

## Success Metrics
- User acquisition and retention rates
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)
- Chatbot performance metrics (response accuracy, user satisfaction)
- System uptime and performance
- Credit usage and billing accuracy
